- Work management
	- allows a software team to plan, track, organize, and review its work
	- typically organized around projects or ongoing tasks
	- project represents a set of work to produce some outcome with a specific scope and within a specific timeframe
	- usually Work Management tools targeted at software teams support an agile workflow with a Kanban board
	- for project work, iterations (sprints) are used to timebox the work
	- ongoing work may use a pure kanban board with the goal of limit amount of work in progress
	- common agile tools like Trello and Asana
- Knowledge Base
	- where they store documentation relevant to their team and project
	- could include team processes, development processes, software design, and architecture and operation processes and instructions
